Michael Landon

Actor, Writer, Director, and Producer

     
       

Michael Landon was well known for his acting roles in Bonanza, Little House on the Prairie, and Highway to Heaven.

1936-1991
(Born as Eugene Maurice Orowitz)

    • Crossroads (three episodes)

    • The Tall Man

  • Major Acting Roles - 1959-1989
    • Bonanza (1959 - 1973) - 14 seasons as Little Joe Cartwright
    • Little House on the Prairie (1974 - 1983) - 9 seasons as Charles Ingalls
    • Highway to Heaven (1984 - 1989) - 5 seasons as Jonathan Smith
